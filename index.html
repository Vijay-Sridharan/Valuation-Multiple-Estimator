Valuation Framework

Valuation Multiple Estimator

A PEG-anchored framework for estimating fair EV / Revenue multiples across Elevar’s portfolio. Adjust inputs to explore scenarios. For founder reference — not investment advice.

Core Methodology
Formula: EV / Revenue = PAT% × Growth% × Narrative Premium / Discount
Anchor Valuation: PE 20× @ 20% growth
PEG ratio: 1.0× → PE = Growth %
Assumes: Net Debt ≈ 0 · No minority interests · Steady-state margin
